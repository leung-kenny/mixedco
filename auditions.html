Who We Are

Mixed Company, Stanford's oldest co-ed a cappella group, performs pop, rock, and everything in between. The group has been featured on several college a cappella compilation albums, including Best of College A Cappella and, most recently, Voices Only 2012. Mixed Company also recently completed their 12th studio album, The Rush, and is currently working on their 13th album!
